# Project notes

## Copy conventions

- **User pronouns in product copy.** In `PROMPT_RULES.md`, its modal display
  (`lib/prompt-rules-content.ts`), and any translations, the generic "user" is
  referred to with masculine singular pronouns — English **he / him / his**,
  Portuguese **ele / seu**. Do **not** use singular "they/them/their" for the
  user; treating the user as "they" is considered a grammar mistake here. Keep
  verb agreement in step (e.g. "what he wants", "code he provides").
